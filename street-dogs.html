What it is

Our house specialty: griddle hot all beef dogs piled high and dressed your way. From the classic to the fully loaded Walking Chili Dog, every dog is built to order with our own street sauce and fresh toppings.

Good for

A fast, hearty lunch, road trip fuel, or a craveable dinner on the patio.

What is included

Each dog is made to order. Add bacon, sauteed onions or peppers, sauerkraut or extra cheese to make it yours.
