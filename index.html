Our Story

A little Homedale gem, run by people who care

Street Dogs BBQ and More is a proud veteran and women owned spot in downtown Homedale. What started as a simple street dog stand has grown into a full lineup of loaded dogs, smashed burgers, Idaho finger steaks and real Southern BBQ, all made fresh to order.

Guests keep coming back for the friendly service, the shaded patio, and food that comes out hot every time. As one reviewer put it, it is a little gem in a little town, worth every penny.

FAQ

Good to know

Does Street Dogs BBQ have a website to order from?

Yes. You can order online any time through our ordering page, or call us at (208) 807-9826. We also serve dine in on the patio, curbside pickup and delivery through DoorDash and Uber Eats.

Where are you located in Homedale?

We are at 2 W Owyhee Ave, Suite B, Homedale, ID 83628, right in the heart of Homedale. Folks drive in from Boise, Caldwell and Nampa for the dogs and BBQ.

Is there a place to sit and eat?

Yes. We have a covered patio with umbrellas and misters so you can dine in comfortably, even on a hot day. You can also grab your order curbside or have it delivered.

What is BBQ Fridays?

We specialize in Southern BBQ and steaks, and the smoker fires up for BBQ Fridays and rotating specials like tri tip, pork ribs and pulled pork. Call ahead to hear what is on the board.

Are you really veteran and women owned?

We are. Street Dogs BBQ and More is a proud veteran and women owned local spot, and we love taking care of our Homedale neighbors and everyone passing through.

What are your most popular items?

Guests rave about the loaded street dogs, the Bacon Ranch Burger, the BBQ nachos, the homemade chili and our Idaho finger steak baskets.
