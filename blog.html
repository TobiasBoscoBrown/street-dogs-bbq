Your first visit to Street Dogs BBQ: what to order and why folks drive in

A friendly guide to the menu, BBQ Fridays and the easiest ways to order

If you have driven through Homedale on US 95, you may have spotted a little spot with a shaded patio, red umbrellas and a steady line of regulars. That is Street Dogs BBQ and More, a veteran and women owned kitchen that has quietly become one of the best reasons to pull off the highway in Owyhee County. People drive in from Boise, Caldwell and Nampa just for the dogs, and once you taste why, you understand the trip.

If it is your first visit, here is everything you need to know: what to order, how our BBQ Fridays work, where to sit, and the easiest ways to grab your food.

Start with the street dogs

We are named for them, so it makes sense to start here. Our street dogs are all beef, griddled hot, and dressed however you like. The Street Dog is our namesake, loaded with the works and our own street sauce. If you want a little heat, the Jalapeno Cheddar Dog brings cheddar and fresh jalapenos. Feeding a bigger appetite? The Double Dog stacks two dogs on one bun.

The one first timers always ask about is the Walking Chili Dog. It is a chili dog served right in the chip bag, so you can eat it on the move. For something classic, the Chicago Dog comes piled with relish, mustard, onion, green pepper, pickle, tomato and sport peppers. There is a reason one traveler told us they had a craving for a juicy dressed up dog all the way back from the Baja.

Do not skip the burgers and baskets

Our "and more" is no afterthought. The smashed burgers are hand pattied and griddled to order, with crispy edges and a soft bun. The Bacon Ranch Burger is the one reviewers single out as juicy to perfection, and the Chili Burger comes smothered in our homemade chili.

Then there are the baskets. If you are not from Idaho, the finger steak basket is a local rite of passage: golden, hand breaded strips of steak, fried fresh. Add a basket of big, hand cut onion rings and you have a meal worth sharing. We also fry up fish and chips, jalapeno cheese sticks and battered mushrooms.

BBQ Fridays and the smoker

We specialize in Southern BBQ and steaks, and that side of the kitchen really comes alive on BBQ Fridays and rotating special days. When the smoker is going, you might find tri tip, pork ribs and pulled pork coming right off the BBQ, plus our famous BBQ nachos piled with smoked pork, cheese, onions and jalapenos. Because specials rotate, the smart move is to call ahead at (208) 807-9826 to hear what is on the board that day.

"Great BBQ and dogs! BBQ sides are uber tasty too. Owners do a great job. Don't miss this little gem in Homedale." Anne D., Yelp

Go loaded when you are hungry

When you want it all, our loaded fries and nachos deliver. The Loaded Street Fries pile sliced hot dog, bacon, sauteed onions, green peppers and cream cheese over fries, all finished with street sauce. The full order of nachos is genuinely built to share, so it is perfect for two. Bring a friend, or do not, we will not judge.

Where to sit, and how to order

Our seating is a covered patio with umbrellas and misters, so it stays comfortable even on a hot Treasure Valley afternoon. One guest summed it up well, calling it a great outdoor area with misters running on a hot day to create the perfect temperature. It is a relaxed, friendly place to slow down for a few minutes.

When it comes to ordering, you have options. You can order online any time through our ordering page, call ahead at (208) 807-9826 so it is ready when you arrive, walk up to the window, grab it curbside, or have it delivered through DoorDash and Uber Eats. Whatever is easiest for you.

"Drove through Homedale on a Tuesday, saw this little hole in the wall restaurant and I have to say the food is excellent. Food is made to order, fresh and hot! Amazing all around." Espy M., Yelp

Why folks keep coming back

At the end of the day, Street Dogs BBQ and More is a small, local, veteran and women owned spot that cares about every plate. Nothing sits under a heat lamp. The dogs are dressed to order, the burgers are smashed fresh, and the BBQ is smoked with patience. Add friendly owners and a shaded patio, and you have the kind of place people go out of their way for.

So next time you are rolling through Homedale, or looking for a reason to take the drive, you know where to go. Browse the full menu, then order online or call us at (208) 807-9826. We will fire it up fresh.
